Background: I shoot and edit a lot of bachata content and got tired of spending 4+ hours manually syncing clips to a track. So I built a tool to automate it.

**What it does:**
- Analyses the audio (BPM, beats, intensity)

- Assembles a beat-synced cut and renders it out
